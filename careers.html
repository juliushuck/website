Core values behaviors

We see every day as an opportunity to turn values into action through our 4 core behaviors.

Slide left
←
Slide right

  1. 1

    Obsess over customer experience

    We deeply understand what we’re building and who we’re building for and serving. We define the leading edge of what’s possible in our industry and deliver the future for our customers.

  2. 2

    Move with heartfelt urgency

    We have a healthy relationship with impatience, channeling it thoughtfully to show up better and faster for our customers and for each other. Time is the most limited thing we have, and we make the most of every moment.

  3. 3

    Say the hard thing with care

    Our best work often comes from intelligent debate, critique, and even difficult conversations. We speak our minds and don’t sugarcoat — and we do so with respect, maturity, and care.

  4. 4

    Make your mark

    We seek out new and unique ways to create meaningful impact, and we champion the same from our colleagues. We work as a team to get the job done, and we go out of our way to celebrate and reward those going above and beyond for our customers and our teammates.

Diversity, equity & inclusion

Part of our mission to empower everyone to create for the web is ensuring that starts with our team. We’re committed to building a diverse, equitable, and inclusive workplace that reflects the world we’re building for.

Remote, together

We’re headquartered in San Francisco — but more than half of our team is remote, working from places like Australia, Lebanon, Poland, Canada, and more. And we work hard to make sure everyone feels involved.

Wellness & benefits

As part of our pursuit to lead impactful, fulfilling lives, we take benefits pretty seriously.

Not only do we offer truly fair and competitive compensation, but we cover 100% of your insurance, give you extra money each month to do more of the things you love, and set you up with the home office of your dreams.

  • Competitive compensation

    Let's face it: In a late capitalism world, getting paid well certainly doesn't hurt.

  • 100% insurance coverage

    For all eligible employees and their dependents, Webflow covers 100% of the health benefits premium for medical, dental, and vision. We also provide a Health Reimbursement Account or Health Savings Account, based on selected insurance plan

  • 401K and financial planning

    Wherever you are in your life, we want to support you in achieving your goals. Whether it’s saving up for a house, planning for a backpacking trip or retirement strategy– we’ve got you. We offer free access to financial planning services to get you where you want to go.

  • Paid time off

    Flexible paid time off — take time off as you need it. Everyone has 10 flexible holidays, paid sick days, and sabbatical leave.

    Generous parental leave, with 12 weeks of child bonding leave for both birthing and non-birthing parents, as well as an additional 6-8 weeks of pregnancy disability for birthing parents to be used before child bonding leave.

  • Annual retreat and offsites

    Love to travel? Us too. We’ll send you on an annual, all-company “working vacation”— plus team-specific offsites. (Pre-COVID) destinations include Mexico and Portugal!

  • 10% time

    Take up to 4 hours per week to focus on building skills, working on projects, or learning new Webflow-related things outside of your daily to-do’s.

  • Commuter benefits

    If you are in a role that requires visits to one of our offices, you are eligible to receive commuter benefits.

  • Vacation bonus

    Get a $1,000 bonus the first time you take 5+ days off. That’s right: We literally pay you to go on vacation.

  • Office setup budget

    Working remotely? Need a desk? We got you. You’ll get up to $500 to use within 90 days of your start date to build out your dream office setup.

    Remote team members only

  • Health and wellness stipend

    You’ll get a monthly stipend to spend on health and wellness. This stipend can cover the cost of a gym membership, meditation app, and other expenses to support a healthy lifestyle.

  • Remote work reimbursements

    Don’t live by the Bay? No prob. Remote Webflowers get a monthly stipend for remote work expenses like essential office supplies, tech gear, and Wi-Fi bills.

    Remote team members only

  • Continued education coverage

    You’ll get a yearly stipend for skill development reimbursements. Use it to get even better at what you do, or learn something brand new to support your career ambitions! This stipend can also be used to cover career coaching if you want it.

  • Choose your own adventure

    So you want to grow in your career, but you don’t want to become a manager? No worries. You can still be an individual contributor and climb in the ranks.

  • Webflow subscription discount

    Get 99% off of any Webflow plan, and a 50% off code to give friends and family.

Geographic pay zones

Because compensation levels may vary within a country, we established cost-of-labor based pay zones for each entity-based country. This means we've assessed the average salary levels for each major metropolitan area in an entity-based country, and grouped those cities/locations with similar average salary levels as one another into the same pay zones.

  • United States

    Zone A

    New York City, San Fransisco, San Jose, Seattle, Jersey City

    Zone B

    Boston, Washington DC, San Diego, Chicago, Los Angeles, Sacramento, Portland, Denver, Austin

    Zone C

    All other U.S. cities

  • United Kingdom

    Zone A

    London

    Zone B

    All other U.K. cities
