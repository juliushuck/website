Introduction to the NoCode tools Toddle, Bubble & Webflow, and some AI

In person
In person
Copenhagen
, 
Denmark
🇩🇰
April
 
17
16:30
 
CEST

Webflow Meetup DK and Kvalifik present... "Introduction to the NoCode tools Toddle, Bubble & Webflow. And some AI." For the first Webflow Copenhagen Meetup of the year, we have once again teamed up with the digital agency Kvalifik, (who are big Webflow enthusiasts) for an afternoon, where we will tour a number of prominent solutions from the NoCode world. We will get to know both: > Toddle – https://toddle.dev/ > Bubble – https://bubble.io/ > Of course we will also go through Webflow ... and a range of tools that can help designers, marketers and others optimize our workflows and efficiency. The event is aimed at both curious NoCode beginners and more experienced NoCode enthusiasts. – Program – ------------ > Toddle: Andreas Møller from Toddle will give us a demo of the NoCode web app tool they've built in ... Toddle. Yep, they have simply built the tool in the very same platform. Pretty impressive feat! Andreas spent 20 years building web applications before founding toddle.dev. ---------- > Bubble: Jesper Vestergaard will give a tour of the NoCode tool Bubble, where he has built a complete app without writing a single line of code. Jesper has spent the past 20 years designing digital products, most recently as a consultant and digital product designer in Stories Without Endings. ---------------------- > NoCode workflows: Riziki Nielsen will present a range of NoCode tools he uses in his daily work as a Digital Designer at our hosts Kvalifik. Riziki is currently involved in designing digital solutions at Kvalifik. -- We look forward to seeing a lot of you for an exciting and educational afternoon!

More information about this event.
