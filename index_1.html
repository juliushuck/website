Useful information

Some frequently asked questions and useful answers.

What services do you offer exactly?

My main focus is on webdesign and Webflow development. Here's a full list of services I provide:

  • Custom webdesign
  • Webflow development
  • Responsive webdesign
  • UIX design
  • Brand design & development
  • Logo design
  • Custom illustrations
I have a small job for you - Do you take on hourly work?

No, sorry, currently I only do fixed-fee projects. However, this does include landingpage/single-page projects.

What are your rates for a full website project?

Most of my projects vary in budget between €8.500,- and €20.000,-. But I can take on larger projects depending on the details of the work. (My minimum engagement fee is €8.500,-)

How long does a project generally take?

This is different for each project, depending on the scope of the work. But generally I aim to finish projects between 4 and 8 weeks.

I already have a design, I just need Webflow development

Yes, I provide this service. With over 10 years of experience in Webflow I can safely say I know my way around. I can work closely with your designer or with the provided designs to translate them into a fully Webflow powered website.

I'm an agency and need a project for my client

Yes, I can collaborate as "white label" for agencies.

I want you to collaborate with my team

Yes this is possible. I can collaborate (closely) with the designers you already have in your team. If the project allows it, we could even arrange a day/days to work together on location to teach the needed basics of managing the website in Webflow.
