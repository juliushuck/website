FAQs

Which plan should I choose?

Webflow offers 2 main types of plans: Workspace plans for collaboration and unhosted site staging, and Site plans for hosting your site with Webflow. When you first sign up to use Webflow, you’ll begin with a free Starter Workspace.

What are the benefits of Webflow Hosting?

All sites hosted on Webflow take advantage of a hosting infrastructure built to handle millions of page views a day, with less than 100ms response time. Sites hosted with Webflow give you a completely managed solution, so you don’t have to worry about applying security patches, downtime, or scalability. We handle it all for you!

How do I connect my custom domain to my Webflow site?

With any Webflow Site plan, you can connect your custom domain to your site and publish it to the web with one click.

Can I use Webflow for free?

You can use Webflow for free, forever, and only purchase a plan to unlock features, or to host and publish your site. No credit card required. No trial period.

Will all elements function on my site if I host the code elsewhere?

CMS, User Accounts, and Ecommerce content (databases) and functionality, as well as Logic flows, aren’t included in exported code. These features require hosting to function as expected. Additionally, site search and forms (including file upload and reCAPTCHA) do not work on exported sites. If you intend to collect form submissions on an exported site, you’ll need to use a third-party tool or embed a third-party integration.

Depending on your Workspace role, you can duplicate, transfer, or delete sites between Workspaces in your Dashboard. We outline the steps in detail in our University lesson.
